Slack Cheat Sheet

About slack

Slack is a communication platform that thrives on organising work interactions, through channels where you can share documents, messages, to do lists, etc.

Since there's a lot of valuable information in their official website here in the form of tutorials, I'll focus on few important aspects.

Managing your Workplace

Managing a workplace can be boiled down to two aspects.

  • Personalising it's looks and organisation.
    • Changing the Workplace picture:

      Start by selecting the Workplace you want to manage on the vertical left dock and then follow the arrows.

      Select Workspace Icon.

      Press Choose File and then Upload Icon.

    • Adding a channel:

    • Renaming or Removing a channel:

      Left click the channel in which you want the perform the operation.

      Select the operation you wish to carry out.

  • Managing the participants.
    • Adding someone to the workplace:

      There are two ways to add someone to your Workplace. Either with through their email or with an invite link.

      Start by selecting the Workplace you want to manage on the vertical left dock and then follow the arrows.

      You'll be presented with the two options.

    • Adding someone to a SPECIFIC channel:

      To add someone to a specific channel select that channel and proceed as follows.

      You'll be asked to select a Workplace member or to send an invite by email.

      By default everyone will be included on all channels.
